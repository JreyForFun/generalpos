# FlexPOS — Quick Start Guide

Welcome to FlexPOS! This guide will get you up and running in under 5 minutes.

---

## 🔑 First Login

1. **Launch FlexPOS** — the app opens to the PIN entry screen
2. **Enter the default PIN:** `1234`
3. **Press Enter** — you'll be logged in as the Admin user

> 💡 **Tip:** Change this PIN immediately after logging in via **Settings → Cashiers**.

---

## 🛒 Your First Sale

1. **Click on a product** in the grid — it's added to the cart on the right
2. Adjust quantities with the **+** / **−** buttons
3. Click the green **Pay** button
4. The payment screen opens — enter the **cash tendered**
5. Click **Confirm Payment**
6. A **receipt preview** appears — click **Print** or **Close**

> 💡 **Keyboard shortcuts:** Press **F2** to focus the product search, **F9** to open payment.

---

## 📦 Adding Products

1. Click **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Product** (top right)
3. Fill in: Name, Price, Category, Stock
4. Click **Create** — the product is now available at checkout

---

## 👥 Managing Customers

1. Click **Customers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Customer**
3. Fill in name, phone, and optional discount info
4. When checking out, click the **customer icon** in the cart to link a customer to the order
5. Linked customers earn **loyalty points** automatically

---

## 📊 Viewing Reports

1. Click **Reports** in the sidebar
2. The daily dashboard shows:
   - Total sales, orders, discounts, tips
   - Sales by payment method
   - Best selling products
   - 7-day sales chart
   - Cashier performance
3. Use the **date picker** to view other days
4. Click **Export PDF** to save a report

---

## ⚙️ Settings

Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar to configure:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Store Name** | Shown on receipts |
| **Store Logo** | Displayed on login screen and receipts |
| **Receipt Note** | Custom footer text for receipts |
| **Loyalty Rate** | Points earned per ₱1 spent |
| **Rounding Mode** | Currency rounding (none, peso, 5 centavos) |
| **Idle Lock** | Auto-lock timeout in seconds |

---

## 💾 Backups

- **Automatic:** The database is backed up automatically when you close the app
- **Manual:** Go to **Reports** → click the **Backup** button to export a copy
- Backups are saved to your user's `AppData/FlexPOS/backups/` folder

---

## 👤 Adding New Cashiers

1. Go to **Cashiers** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Cashier**
3. Set a name, PIN (minimum 4 digits), and role:
   - **Cashier** — can process sales and view orders
   - **Manager** — can also manage products and refund orders
   - **Admin** — full access to all features

---

## 🎁 Gift Cards

1. Go to **Gift Cards** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Gift Card** — set a balance and optional expiry
3. At checkout, use the **gift card payment** option to redeem

---
